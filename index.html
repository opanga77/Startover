Poa charcoal

Poa charcoal is a mordern charcoal made from sample waste papers and organic materials. It's environmental friendly with smoke free and long lasting,the product is also affordable. We sell our product according to the quantity.

Requirements

  • Scrap paper.
  • Bucket.
  • Organic material(i.e sawdust,pine needle or leaves.)
  • 2-litre sode bottle.
  • Plastic.
  • Can of food(i.e can of bean.)

Here are the steps/procedure...

  • Put on an overal or old cloth.
  • Wear gloves.
  • Shred scrap papers into small pieces.
  • Soak it for a few days.
  • Work your paper into poridge-like paste...
  • Add in organic material...
  • Add water...
  • Mold your briquette and dry for a few days(three to seven days).
